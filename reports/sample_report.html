How We Actually Do This

Most intelligence platforms scrape the internet and call it a day. We don't.

Step 1: Founders keep us updated (gamified system)

We built a system where founders WANT to update their profiles. Why? Because investors using Lemina actively search for companies. The more complete your profile, the more visibility you get. Founders compete to be featured. We get fresh data. You get accurate intelligence.

Step 2: We collect what investors actually need

Not vanity metrics. Real data: Monthly burn rate. Runway. Customer acquisition cost. Churn. Revenue (not GMV). Active users (not downloads). We ask for the questions YOU would ask in due diligence.

Step 3: Multi-source verification

Founder says "$50K MRR"? We verify with: payment processor data (where available), team size (salary burn implies revenue), LinkedIn activity, customer testimonials, regulatory filings. If we can't verify, we flag it.

Step 4: Market sizing using real data

Not "Nigeria has 200M people, therefore TAM is $5B." We calculate bottom-up: How many companies in this sector? Average revenue per company? Growth rates? Market saturation? You get TAM/SAM/SOM models built from actual company data, not fantasy.

Plus: Investment Readiness Scores

We score every company on capital efficiency, growth trajectory, and runway. You see at a glance: BUY (strong fundamentals, good entry point), HOLD (wait for better metrics), or AVOID (red flags present).
